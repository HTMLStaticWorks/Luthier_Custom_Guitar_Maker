The Importance of Grain

Grain direction isn't just about looks. For an acoustic soundboard, the tightness of the grain determines the stiffness-to-weight ratio, which directly impacts the projection and volume of the instrument.

We only use Master Grade timbers, hand-split to ensure that the grain is perfectly perpendicular to the surface for maximum structural integrity.
