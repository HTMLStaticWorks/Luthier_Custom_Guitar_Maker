Craftsmanship Beyond Boundaries

We believe that a guitar is more than just wood and wire. It's an extension of the artist.

From the initial rough-cut of the mahogany to the final spray of nitrocellulose lacquer, our focus is entirely on the sonic properties and structural integrity of each build. We don't just build guitars; we curate experiences.

  • Hand-tuned soundboards for maximum resonance.
  • Custom-wound pickups for signature clarity.
  • Sustainable wood sourcing with full traceability.
