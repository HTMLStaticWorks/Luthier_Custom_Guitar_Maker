01

Design & Timber Selection

The journey begins with a conversation. We discuss your playing style, tonal preferences, and aesthetic desires. Once the blueprint is drawn, we spend hours in our wood library finding the perfect match of grain, density, and moisture content.

02

Rough Cutting & Carving

The wood is rough-cut and allowed to settle. Then, the real magic happens. Using hand gouges and precision templates, we carve the arches and the neck profile. This is where the ergonomics of the guitar are born—ensuring it feels natural in your hands.

03

Fretting & Assembly

The neck is mated to the body using traditional joinery. Each fret is hand-pressed, leveled, and polished to a mirror shine. The electronics are wired by hand using silver solder and top-tier components for maximum signal integrity.

04

Finishing & Voicing

The final step is the most patient. We apply multiple thin layers of nitrocellulose or oil, allowing the wood to breathe. Finally, we string it up, adjust the action to your exact specs, and "voice" the instrument—a final adjustment that brings out its unique character.
